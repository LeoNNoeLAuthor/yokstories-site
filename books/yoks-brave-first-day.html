For parents, teachers, and librarians

What this story practices

Yok’s Brave First Day gives children one concrete, repeatable way to practice school readiness without turning the story into a lecture.

  • Put one thing where it belongs.
  • Say one small hello.
  • Choose one gentle activity to begin.
