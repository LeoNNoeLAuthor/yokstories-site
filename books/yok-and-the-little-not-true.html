For parents, teachers, and librarians

What this story practices

Yok and the Little Not-True gives children one concrete, repeatable way to practice honesty without turning the story into a lecture.

  • Stop and take one breath.
  • Tell what really happened.
  • Say sorry if someone or something was hurt.
