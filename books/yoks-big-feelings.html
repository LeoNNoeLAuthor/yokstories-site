For parents, teachers, and librarians

What this story practices

Yok’s Big Feelings gives children one concrete, repeatable way to practice big feelings without turning the story into a lecture.

  • Notice the feeling in your body.
  • Name the feeling if you can.
  • Take one slow breath and ask for what you need.
