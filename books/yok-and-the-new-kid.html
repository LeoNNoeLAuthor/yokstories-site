For parents, teachers, and librarians

What this story practices

Yok and the New Kid gives children one concrete, repeatable way to practice inclusion without turning the story into a lecture.

  • Notice who is standing apart.
  • Make space nearby.
  • Offer one gentle invitation and wait kindly.
