For parents, teachers, and librarians

What this story practices

Yok’s First Friend gives children one concrete, repeatable way to practice friendship without turning the story into a lecture.

  • Say one small hello or invitation.
  • Share a toy or take turns with support.
  • Give a shy child time to join at their pace.
