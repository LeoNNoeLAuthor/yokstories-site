For parents, teachers, and librarians

What this story practices

Yok Learns to Wait gives children one concrete, repeatable way to practice waiting without turning the story into a lecture.

  • Give hands a waiting job.
  • Count something nearby.
  • Cheer for someone else while your turn is coming.
